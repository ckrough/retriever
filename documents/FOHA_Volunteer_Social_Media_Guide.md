# FOHA Volunteer Social Media Guide

Promote FOHA pets for adoption!

## Facebook: Share the Love!

### Where to Share:

- **FOHA_VA Public Group:** This is where potential adopters are!
- **Private Staff/Volunteer Group:** For internal staff/volunteer communication only.

### Tips for Success:

- Share updates, photos, videos and adoption info about a FOHA dog or cat!
- Include the link to the dog or cat's foha.org bio page in the post or in the comments.
- Keep the tone **positive** and **upbeat** â€“ let their personality shine!

## Instagram: Tag & Foster!

### Instagram Guidelines:

- **Tag Us:** Always tag @foha_va in your posts and stories.
- **FOHA Account:** Consider creating a dedicated Instagram account for your favorite FOHA dogs and/or cats! But continue to tag @foha_va!

### Why Create your Own FOHA Account?

- Increased Visibility: More eyes on available FOHA dogs and cats!
- Dedicated Content: Show off their unique personality.

## FOHA Dog Walker App

Use the app to upload new photos or videos, or check out other volunteer's notes!

- **Access:** fohadogs.adalo.com
- **Upload:** Share your best photos directly through the app. Photos are now automatically embedded into a dog or cat's foha.org bio page!

## FOHA.org: Bio Updates

### How to Comment on Your Foster's Bio:

1. **Account Creation:** Create an account on foha.org.
2. **Comment Away:** Add heartwarming stories and observations to your foster's bio.
3. **Admin Approval:** Wait for administrator approval (this is essential!). Allow 24-48 hours.
4. **To post videos:** You will need to have a YouTube account, then you can post the link to the video in the comment section.

*A personal touch can make all the difference!*

## Marketing & Photography Tips: Capture Hearts!

### Photography:

- **Natural Light:** Avoid harsh shadows and use natural light whenever possible.
- **Focus on Eyes:** Sharp focus on the eyes creates connection.
- **Clear Backgrounds:** Minimize distractions behind your subject.
- **Get on Pet's Level:** Capture their perspective.
- **Show Variety:** Capture pets sitting, playing, and sleeping.

### Marketing:

- **Joyful Expressions:** Capture pets looking happy, playful, and relaxed.
- **Upbeat Captions:** Highlight their best qualities and quirks.
- **Be specific:** Talk about their favorite thing, or a funny antic they did!
- **Ask Questions:** Encourage engagement to reach more users.

## Summary

This guide has provided you with the know-how for promoting FOHA's adoptable animals on social media and foha.org. By following these tips, you can enhance their visibility, capture their unique personalities, and ultimately, help them find their forever homes!
